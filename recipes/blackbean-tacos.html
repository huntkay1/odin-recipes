Black Bean Tacos

Description

These tacos are quick, easy, and oh-so delicious! The smoky, flavorful filling whips up in minutes. Pair them with your favorite salsa!

  • canned black beans
  • canned chipotle in adobo sauce
  • sliced onion
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • oregano
  • chicken broth
  • tortillas
  • cotija cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Grease a sheet pan and set aside.
  3. In a saute pan, add black beans, onion, and chopped chipotle peppers. Allow to saute until onion is translucent.
  4. Add chicken broth and mash beans until a refried bean consistency.
  5. Add spices and a spoon full of the adobo sauce and mix in completley.
  6. Fill the toritillas with the black bean filling, sprinkle cheese on top, and fold in half, pressing the sides together.
  7. Line tacos on a baking sheet and add another baking sheet on top.
  8. Place the tacos in the oven and bake for 10 minutes. After, flip the tacos and bake for another 10 minutes.
  9. Serve with your favorite salsa for dipping.
