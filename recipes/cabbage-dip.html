Cabbage Dip

Description

This cabbage dip is a fresh and healthy way to snack! It's quick and easy and guaranteed to be a huge hit. I promise that you won't be able to get enough

  • cabbage
  • cucumber
  • green onion
  • garlic
  • shallot
  • lemon juice
  • rice wine vinegar
  • spinach
  • basil
  • walnuts
  • cashews
  • nutritional yeast
  • tortilla chips
  1. Finely chop cabbage, cucumber, and green onion and place in a large bowl
  2. Blend together the rest of the ingredients until smooth.
  3. Pour the sauce over the cabbage mixture and mix well
  4. Serve with tortilla chips.
