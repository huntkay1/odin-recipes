Zuppa Toscana

Description

Zuppa toscana is a broad term, literally meaning "Tuscan Soup". It contains potatoes, sausage, and kale in a spicy, creamy broth base. Zuppa toscana is the perfect delicious cure for a chilly day!

  • potatoes - diced
  • italian sausage
  • kale
  • onion - diced
  • garlic - minced
  • heavy cream
  • chicken broth
  • red chili flakes
  • salt and pepper
  • parmesean cheese
  1. Brown the italian sausage in a stock pot.
  2. Remove the sausage and set aside. Drain most of the grease from the pot until about 2 tablespoons of grease is left.
  3. Add onion and saute until transluscent. Add onion and saute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add in potatoes. Cook until fork tender.
  6. Once potatoes are cooked, add in heavy cream and red chili flakes.
  7. Allow the soup to heat up again and add kale. Stir until the kale is wilted.
  8. Finish with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with grated parmesean.
